##![basic](https://raw.github.com/massiveart/sulu-docs/master/system-requirements/images/basic.png)1200 Resource Locator Path Management
####![Alpha](https://raw.github.com/massiveart/sulu-docs/master/system-requirements/images/alpha.png)FR-1201 Strategies

Facilities shall be provided to support the following resource locator management strategies:

* as short as possible
* view the whole tree
* configurable per portal

####![Alpha](https://raw.github.com/massiveart/sulu-docs/master/system-requirements/images/alpha.png)FR-1202 History

The system shall provide following historization functionalities:

* automatic redirects of old URLs (301)
* URL history list view of pages since first creation including page ranks, online period and creator
* archive and restore entries


####![Alpha](https://raw.github.com/massiveart/sulu-docs/master/system-requirements/images/alpha.png)FR-1203 Prefixes

The system shall support prefixes for mapping complex site structures (languages, segmentations like seasons etc.).
